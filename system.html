The System

One Controller.
Every Light.

AURACORE is a single connected lighting system, not a box of loose parts. One controller drives all of your addressable RGBW lighting — reflectors, and more as you add them — so every light moves, fades, and changes color together.

01

Plug in the harness

Our harnesses sit inline with your factory connectors. No cutting, no splicing, no soldering into your car — and it all comes back to stock in minutes if you ever want it to.

02

Add the controller

The AuraCore controller is the brain. One unit powers and runs your whole setup, so you don't need a separate module for every light you add.

03

Pick your look

Choose solid colors, whites, or animated effects across every light at once. Change it whenever you want — your setup grows with you as you add more AURACORE parts.
